A. Procedure:

  1. Booking Form Submission:
    • Read the rules and regulations carefully.
    • Fill out the booking form completely, ensuring that your details (name, ID, contact info, etc.) are accurate.
    • Submit the completed booking form.
  2. Lab Access:
    • Once you receive confirmation of equipment availability, proceed to the lab on the agreed-upon time.
    • Check-in at the lab entrance and ensure you're aware of any specific protocols or instructions for using the equipment.

B. Rules and Regulations:

  1. Equipment Borrowing Procedure:
    • All requests to borrow equipment must be made in advance by filling out the official Equipment Borrowing Form.
    • Requests should be submitted at least 24 hours before the desired pickup time to allow for processing and preparation.
    • Approval is subject to equipment availability and the borrower's standing with the lab.
  2. Borrowing Duration:
    • The standard borrowing duration for most equipment is 5 days.
    • An extension for additional days may be requested online or in person before the original due date. Extensions are subject to availability and are not guaranteed.
  3. Booking Cancellation:
    • If you no longer need the equipment you reserved, please cancel your request at least 12 hours in advance to make the equipment available to others.
  4. Lab Equipment Usage:
    • All lab equipment must be handled with care and used for academic purposes only.
    • The borrower is fully responsible for the equipment from the moment it is checked out until it is successfully returned and inspected by lab staff.
    • Any damage, malfunction, or loss of equipment must be reported to the lab supervisor immediately. The borrower will be financially responsible for the full cost of repair or replacement.
  5. Late Returns:
    • Equipment returned after the due date will incur late penalties.
    • Repeated failure to return items on time may result in the suspension of your borrowing privileges for the rest of the academic year.
  6. Equipment Return:
    • Equipment must be returned in person to a lab staff member during lab operating hours.
    • Please ensure all components, cables, chargers, and accessories are returned with the main piece of equipment.
    • Allow for a few minutes upon return for lab staff to inspect the equipment's condition.
